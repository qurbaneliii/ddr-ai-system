# Task Interpretation

The primary objective is a credible local MVP, not a claim of production drilling-engineering validation. Exact facts are extracted deterministically with provenance. Ollama is the primary local query-understanding and grounded-verbalization provider; lexical retrieval is the explicit offline fallback. The local model cannot overwrite source facts. The supplied corpus is digital PDF-first; OCR is a fallback. Plot results are candidate-level evidence with inspectable overlays.
